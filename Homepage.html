Why PAAIS?

This is a summit dedicated exclusively to the African AI ecosystem. From indigenous LLMs to sovereign data infrastructure. If it’s AI, it lives here!

Building on the 1st Pan African AI Summit in 2025, in 2026, you will attend technical masterclasses to experience live code reviews of African NLP models. You will dissect case studies on deploying ML in low-bandwidth environments such as villages.

Join 1,000+ attendees including: ML Engineers | Data Scientists | Policy Architects | AI Ethicists | AI Startups | Business Leaders | Developers | Venture Capitalists. Don't just talk about AI and the future. Learn how to build or use it.

Scale

Implement validated AI models into practical national and regional initiatives..

Showcase

Showcase youth-led AI innovation through live demos, solutions expo, and mentorship labs.

Advance

Advance ethical AI frameworks with policymakers, technologists, and civil society.

Build

Understand data sovereignty, biases in African datasets, and infrastructure. Build large language models (LLM) with Afrocentric data for relevant local solutions.

Empower

Establish lasting partnerships. Whether it's for your startup, hiring pipeline for your team or getting a draft framework for your organisation's AI policy. Achieve tangible ROI for your participation.

Partner

Enable Africa's youth to become technical powerhouse, eliminating the friction between talent and opportunity. Provide a high-signal platform where young engineers demonstrate elite competence in real-time.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is PAAIS 2026?

The Pan African AI & Innovation Summit (PAAIS) is Africa's premier gathering for AI technologists, policymakers, and innovators. In 2026, we focus on scaling ethical AI ecosystems, building youth capacity, and fostering strategic partnerships across the continent.

Where is the summit being held?

The 2026 summit will be held at the prestigious Kempinski Hotel Gold Coast City in Accra, Ghana. The event dates are September 22nd – 23rd, 2026.

How can I apply to be a speaker?

We welcome expert voices in AI Ethics, NLP, Data Sovereignty, and Innovation. You can apply by clicking the "View More" button in the speakers section or by contacting us directly at info@panafricanaisummit.com.

Are there exhibition opportunities?

Absolutely! The PAAIS Innovation Expo allows startups and established tech companies to showcase their latest AI solutions to a global audience of over 1,000 attendees.

Is there support for international attendees?

Yes, we provide visa support letters for registered delegates. Once your registration is confirmed, our team will assist you with the necessary documentation for your travel to Ghana.
